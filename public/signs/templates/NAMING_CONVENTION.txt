ROAD SIGNS IMAGE NAMING CONVENTION
====================================

Use these EXACT filenames when adding images to the system:

REGULATORY SIGNS (place in regulatory/ folder):
- stop-sign.png
- yield-sign.png
- speed-limit.png
- no-entry.png
- one-way.png
- no-parking.png
- no-stopping.png
- no-left-turn.png
- no-right-turn.png
- do-not-pass.png

WARNING SIGNS (place in warning/ folder):
- curve-ahead.png
- winding-road.png
- steep-hill.png
- slippery-when-wet.png
- road-narrows.png
- deer-crossing.png
- pedestrian-crossing.png
- school-zone-ahead.png
- railway-crossing.png
- low-clearance.png

GUIDE & INFORMATION SIGNS (place in guide/ folder):
- hospital.png
- gas-station.png
- rest-area.png
- exit-sign.png
- service-centre.png

SCHOOL & PEDESTRIAN SIGNS (place in school/ folder):
- Add school-related signs here as needed

TEMPORARY/CONSTRUCTION SIGNS (place in temporary/ folder):
- Add construction signs here as needed

FILE FORMATS ACCEPTED:
- .png (recommended for transparency)
- .jpg
- .gif

RECOMMENDED IMAGE SIZE:
- 256x256 pixels
- 512x512 pixels (for high resolution)

IMPORTANT:
- Use lowercase letters and hyphens only
- No spaces in filenames
- Match the exact sign ID from the system
- Images will automatically replace the generated CSS visuals
